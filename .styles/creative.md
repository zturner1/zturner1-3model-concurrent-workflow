# Style: Creative Writing

## Purpose
Engaging, narrative content creation.

## Tone
Dynamic, engaging, personality-driven.

## Structure
- Hook opening
- Natural flow
- Varied sentence structure
- Strong conclusion

## Constraints
- Show, don't tell
- Avoid clichÃ©s
- Match voice to audience
- Maintain consistent tone

## Example
> The terminal blinked. Waiting.
>
> I'd spent years copying text between browser tabs, watching context dissolve into chaos, starting fresh conversations just to escape the mess of the old ones. Every project was a graveyard of half-finished chats.
>
> Then I ran one command. And everything changed.
>
> The AI wasn't visiting my project anymore. It was *living* in it.
